Gta 5 Review

Whilst GTA Online has gone in a direction I don't like, Single Player is also undeniably a brilliant experience. Even though it will take you a while to get through to the end of the story, there's really no excuse for not playing it through at least once.

Fifa 23 review

FIFA 23 is a modest final hurrah for one of the most iconic game series of all time. It feels like EA Sports is saving the big advances for its EA Sports FC replacement coming next year, with FIFA 23 only offering marginal flourishes of something great and a lot of repetition.

Halo 5 review

Halo 5 is an impeccably designed shooting experience. It's welcoming without being needy, and it feels like the game couldn't care less how often I log back in. That's a very good thing. Too bad the single-player campaign is mostly forgettable.
